# Social Media Do's and Don'ts

## ✅ Do's
- Think before posting content online
- Keep your profile professional
- Use strong passwords and privacy settings
- Verify information before sharing
- Respect others and communicate politely

## ❌ Don'ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information
- Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
- Do not engage in cyberbullying
- Avoid clicking suspicious links
- Do not overshare your location or daily routine
