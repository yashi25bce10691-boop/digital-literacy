# Stay Safe Online Checklist

- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
- Always verify the sender before sharing personal information
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible
- Never share OTPs or banking details with anyone

## UPI / Financial Safety
- Double-check UPI IDs before sending money
- Do not accept unknown payment requests
- Avoid scanning random QR codes

## Reporting Cybercrime
- Report incidents at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call helpline number: 1930
